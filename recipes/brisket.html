Brisket

Ingredients

  • 1 4-5 lb beef brisket
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup paprika
  • 2 Tbsp salt
  • 1 Tbsp black pepper
  • 1 Tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 Tbsp onion powder
  • 1 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 Tbsp cumin
  • 1 Tbsp cayenne pepper

Instructions

  1. Combine all the spices in a small bowl and mix well.
  2. Rub the spice mixture all over the brisket, making sure to coat all sides.
  3. Wrap the brisket in plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  4. Preheat oven to 300Â°F. Place the brisket in a roasting pan and cover with foil.
  5. Cook for 3-4 hours, or until the brisket is fork-tender.
