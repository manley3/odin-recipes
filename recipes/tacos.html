Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 6 taco shells
  • 1 cup lettuce, shredded
  • 1 cup tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, cook the beef over medium heat until browned. Add the onion and garlic and cook until the onion is translucent.
  2. Add the taco seasoning and cook according to the package instructions.
  3. Divide the beef mixture among the taco shells and top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.
