Pizza

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups warm water (110°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) of active dry yeast
  • 3 1/2 cups bread flour
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar

Instructions

  1. Pour warm water into a bowl and sprinkle with yeast. Let sit for 5 minutes until the yeast is dissolved.
  2. Combine flour and salt in a large mixing bowl. Make a well in the center and add the water and yeast mixture. Use a spoon to mix until a shaggy dough forms.
  3. Once the flour is mostly hydrated, turn the dough out onto a clean work surface and knead for 10-15 minutes. The dough should be soft, smooth, and bouncy.
  4. Divide the dough into 3 equal portions and place in lightly oiled bowls. Cover and let rise for 1-2 hours, or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat oven to 550°F (or the highest setting). On a floured surface, stretch each portion of dough into a large circle. Top with your favorite toppings and bake for 8-10 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown.
